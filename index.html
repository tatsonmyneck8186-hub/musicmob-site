FAQ

CLEAR TERMS. NO WEIRD SURPRISES.

What do I get for $9.99?

You receive one custom song concept delivered as an MP3 to the email you provide. The song is created from your submitted idea, genre, and reference direction.

Do I pay before or after submitting my idea?

Submit the form first. After your request is sent, you are redirected to Stripe checkout. Production begins once payment is completed.

Can I use a reference artist?

Yes. Reference artists are used only to understand vibe, tempo, genre, or energy. We do not copy protected songs, lyrics, melodies, voices, or artist identities.

Who owns the song?

You keep full rights to your finished custom song, including the ability to release, monetize, and earn royalties from it. MusicMob may use short previews for portfolio or marketing unless you request privacy by email.

Are refunds available?

Because each song is custom work, completed orders are generally non-refundable once production starts. If we cannot complete your order, we will contact you about a replacement or refund.

Do you allow offensive or illegal content?

No. We may refuse requests involving threats, harassment, explicit illegal activity, impersonation, or content that violates platform/payment rules.

This FAQ is customer information, not legal advice. For commercial releases, publishing, samples, or rights questions, consult a qualified professional.
